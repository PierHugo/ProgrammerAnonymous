We are here to help people that are addicted to programming.

Programmer Anonymous (PA) is an international mutual aid fellowship with the stated purpose of enabling its members to "stay away from coding and help other programmers achieve this goal." PA is nonprofessional, self-supporting, and apolitical. Its only membership requirement is a desire to stop programming. The PA program of recovery is set forth in the Twelve Steps.
